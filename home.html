  • Full-to-Full: The car is provided with a full tank of fuel and must be returned with a full tank. This is generally the most cost-effective option.

  • Pre-Purchase: You pay for a full tank of fuel at the time of rental and can return the car with any level of fuel. This option is convenient if you don't want to worry about refueling before returning the car, but may be more expensive.

  • Full-to-Empty: You pay for a full tank of fuel at the time of rental and return the car empty. Any unused fuel will not be refunded.
